How to Play

  1. Player Setup:
    • Both players begin by entering their names in the input fields.
    • Click the Next button to proceed to the next step.
  2. Category Selection :
    • You and your opponent will choose a common category from a list (e.g., General Knowledge, Science, Sports, Food etc.).
    • Once a category is selected, click Start Quiz to fetch the questions.
  3. Question Round:
    • The game has 6 questions from the selected category:
      • 2 Easy Questions (10 points each)
      • 2 Medium Questions (15 points each)
      • 2 Hard Questions (20 points each)
    • The players will take turns answering questions:
      • Player 1 answers the first easy question.
      • Player 2 answers the second easy question.
      • This pattern continues for medium and hard questions.
  4. Answering Questions:
    • For each question, you will be shown a multiple-choice set of answers.
    • Choose your answer carefully! You can only select one option.
    • Once an answer is selected, the correct answer is revealed, and points are awarded based on the difficulty of the question.
  5. Scoring
    • Easy Question: 10 points for a correct answer.
    • Medium Question: 15 points for a correct answer.
    • Hard Question: 20 points for a correct answer.
  6. End of the Round:
    • After all 6 questions from the chosen category are answered, the players have two options:
      • Select another category and repeat the question round (previously chosen categories are disabled).
      • End the game and proceed to the results page.
  7. Game End and Results:
    • The game ends when either all categories are exhausted or players choose to finish early.
    • The final scores are displayed, and the player with the highest score is declared the winner.
    • If both players have the same score, it results in a draw.

Rules and Regulations

To ensure a fair and fun gameplay experience, please follow these rules and regulations:

  1. Player Turn
    • Players must take turns answering questions.
    • Player 1 always answers the first question, followed by Player 2, and alternates continue for each question type (easy, medium, hard).
  2. Correct Answers
    • Players must select only one answer per question. Once selected, it cannot be changed.
    • Correct answers will be highlighted after the player selects an option.
  3. Points Distribution
    • Points are awarded based on the difficulty of the question as mentioned above.
    • No points are deducted for incorrect answers, but the turn will move to the next player.
  4. Category Selection
    • Both players must agree on the category before starting the round.
    • Categories can only be chosen once. After a category has been used, it will be disabled and unavailable for future rounds.
  5. Game Completion
    • The game can be ended manually by selecting the option to end the game after a round.
    • The game can also be ended automatically when all available categories have been used.
  6. Winner Declaration
    • The player with the highest score at the end of the game is declared the winner.
    • In the case of a tie (equal scores), the game results in a draw.
  7. Replay
    • Players can choose to restart the game and play again with new categories or the same category.

Game Tips

  • Strategy: Since harder questions are worth more points, saving your best guesses for medium and hard rounds can boost your score.
  • Time Management: If using the timer, keep an eye on the clock. Make sure to read all answer options before time runs out.
  • Category Knowledge: Pick categories that both you and your opponent know well for an exciting and balanced challenge.
  • Have Fun!: This game is meant to be fun and challenging, so enjoy playing and testing your knowledge!
